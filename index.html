FAQs

Which health board serves my area?

Where does the data come from?

From the Public Health Wales website.

How often is the data updated?

Every day in line with the Public Health Wales website

Why does the amount of data on this page vary each day?

The data released by Public Health Wales varies on a day by day basis. This page will always present as much data as possible. If the government decide to release historical data, some visualisations such as a timeline may be added to this page.

Who made this page and why?

Rhys Streefland, a full stack developer based in London. You can find me on GitHub and LinkedIn. I created this page as I found it difficult to find information about cases online with the same level of granularity as the information about England.
